Integration of Intelligence
Event: Locusts appear — follow swarms, defeat Gnoster variant
Triggers when Nightlord is: Gnoster
Buff: Attacks have a rare chance to harvest Runes from enemies on hit.
🫧
Unifying Fate
Event: Giant Bubbles forming — step into bubbles, defeat lesser Augur
Triggers when Nightlord is: Gnoster, Caligo, or Heolstor
Buff: Flasks of Crimson Tears also restore FP in addition to HP.
🕷️
Favor of the Night
Event: Purple orb in sky — kill all Fingercreepers & variants
Triggers when Nightlord is: Gnoster, Libra, or Harmonia (DLC)
Buff: Ultimate Art Gauge charges significantly faster for the rest of the expedition.
👁️
Traces of Grace-Given Lord
Event: Fell Omen appears on map — defeat it before it kills you
Triggers when Nightlord is: Caligo, Fulghor, or Heolstor
Buff: Attack power increases each time you interact with a newly found Site of Grace.
😈
Demon's Plating
Event: "A demon casts its curse" — curse halves max HP; pay 15 k / 35 k Runes to lift it (one player pays for all)
Triggers when Nightlord is: Gladius, Maris, or Fulghor
Buff: All players gain Demon's Plating — a defensive buff lasting the entire expedition. Also grants a free wish with no downside.
⚔️
Rise from Near-Death (DLC)
Event: Balancers raid — 7 valkyries teleport your team; each kill restores 1 Flask, failure costs all Flasks for the Night
Triggers when Nightlord is: Balancers / Harmonia (DLC only)
Buff: Rare chance to automatically rise from near-death (like Wending Grace). Attack power is permanently boosted for the rest of the run after rising. One of the strongest buffs in the game.
