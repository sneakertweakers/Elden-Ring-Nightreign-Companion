🏕️ Evergoal ATK Scaling (Battlefield Experience)

Completing Evergoals (on-map camp objectives cleared during Night 1 & Night 2) grants a stacking Battlefield Experience buff. Each Evergoal adds another layer of ATK power for the rest of the expedition. The bonus is multiplicative with relic ATK, invasion buffs, and weapon passives — making early Evergoal clearing extremely valuable.

What are Weapon Passives? Certain legendary and found weapons carry a unique passive ability separate from their Weapon Art. These effects are always active while the weapon is in your inventory (you don't need to have it equipped in hand). They do NOT occupy a relic slot. Weapon passives stack with relics and Evergoal buffs but cannot be duplicated (you can't carry two of the same named weapon).

📋 What Stacks / What Doesn't

  • Same relic effect, multiple copies — ADD together. Two Grand Physical ATK Up relics = +20% total physical ATK.
  • Different ATK buff types — MULTIPLY together. Physical ATK Up × Fire ATK Up × Evergoal ATK × Low HP ATK all multiply for massive combined bonuses.
  • Evergoal ATK (Battlefield Experience) — Stacks MULTIPLICATIVELY with all relic effects, invasion buffs, and weapon passives.
  • Invasion buffs — All stack with relics, Evergoal ATK, and weapon passives.
  • Near Sleep + Near Madness — Both can be active simultaneously as they are separate buff entries.
  • Weapon passives + relics — Always multiplicative with each other. Low HP ATK Up (relic) × Bloodboon Ritual (weapon) is legal and extremely strong.
  • Different elemental ATK Up relics — Each element (Fire, Magic, Lightning, Holy) is tracked separately so all four can be active simultaneously.
  • Same weapon-type bonus (3+ weapons) — Only one bonus per weapon type. You can have TWO different weapon-type bonuses active (e.g. Greatswords + Katanas), not three.
  • Hit-Back Rage (timed buff) — Refreshes its duration on additional hits taken; does NOT double the ATK bonus. Duration is capped at 10 s.
  • FP Cost Reduction (after Char. Skill) — Two Delicate copies extend the window. A Grand copy makes it global, making additional Delicate copies redundant.
  • Boss Relics (Night of…) — Cannot equip the same Boss Relic twice. Each is unique per expedition.
  • Weapon passives (duplicate weapon) — You cannot carry two copies of the same named weapon. No way to double a weapon passive.
  • Undying Flame — Triggers once per expedition regardless of how many weapons with this passive you carry.
  • Spectral companions (Wraith Walking, Ancestral Ward, Corpse Raising) — Each caps at one active entity. Multiple spectral-type weapon passives do NOT spawn additional companions simultaneously.
  • Invasion buff: Rise from Near-Death — The auto-revive triggers once per player per Night. Having higher ATK after it triggers does stack with everything else.
